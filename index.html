What is Hidden Practice?

Hidden Practice is a Longevity and Performance app which gives you one Daily Story with tools, strategies and tasks within mental, emotional, physical and spiritual development, seeking to transform your inner narrative for personal development which enables you to make sustainable change in your habits leading to a better life and a longer life.

How long is the course?

Actually, this is not a course and you can use the app for as long as you decide. We provide you with bits of information and comprehensive guidelines within all of these areas of development which you are gradually learning and implementing in your own pace.

You set the targets for what you want to achieve and we help you through the process of creating sustainable change and not temporary change.

What is the purpose?

The purpose is to enable your sustainable change of habits that benefits your longevity and your performance in life.
